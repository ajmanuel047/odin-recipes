Indomie-Jollof

Description

The above image is the finished product of a Nigerian indomie-jollof

It is very easy to make as long as you have the right ingredients

ingredients

  • Indomie
  • Raw Tomatoes
  • Tin Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Green Peas

Steps

Below are the steps to prepare this meal

  1. Start boiling your Eggs
  2. Put water into the pot and put it on fire
  3. When it starts boiling you add the Indomie
  4. As soon as the indomie becomes a bit soft you add your sliced indomie and the green peas
  5. After this you add the sliced raw tomatoes and the tin Tomatoes
  6. Finally add a little groundnut oil
  7. Stir and when it dries up your food is ready
